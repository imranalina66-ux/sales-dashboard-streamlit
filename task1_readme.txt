# Sales Data Analysis - Task 1

## Steps Performed:
1. Data Cleaning:
   - Removed rows with missing product names (if any)
   - Converted 'Order Date' to datetime format
   - Standardized column names

2. Data Analysis:
   - Calculated monthly total sales
   - Identified top 5 best-selling products
   - Grouped sales by region to compare performance

3. Visualizations:
   - Line chart: Monthly sales trend
   - Bar chart: Top products by revenue
   - Pie chart: Sales share by region

## Key Insights:
- Sales have remained steady overall with small fluctuations.
- Product sales distribution shows some products are consistently higher.
- Regional sales contribution is evenly spread with slight dominance in some areas.
